Marriage Events

Celebrating the union of two hearts

Haldi
A joyful ceremony where turmeric paste is applied to the bride and groom to bless and purify them before the wedding.
Mehendi
The bride’s hands and feet are adorned with intricate henna designs during a fun gathering with music and laughter.
Sangeet
A musical night of dance, performances, and celebration by both families, marking the joy of the union.
Wedding
The sacred ceremony where the couple exchanges vows and rituals are performed to unite them in marriage.
Reception
A grand party hosted by the groom’s family to introduce the bride to extended friends and relatives.
